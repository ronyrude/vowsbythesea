Florida Gulf Coast Beach Wedding - Larkin & Kate

August 29, 2024 · Florida Gulf Coast

Larkin and Kate traveled all the way from Maine to say their vows along Florida’s Gulf Coast, and their wedding was nothing short of magical. With Vows By The Sea carefully planning every detail, the couple enjoyed a seamless and romantic beachfront ceremony framed by soft white sands and the sparkling ocean.

The intimate gathering captured everything they dreamed of in a destination wedding—the sound of waves, the Florida sunshine, and a golden sunset to seal the moment they became husband and wife.

Anna Parker Photography documented the day with stunning artistry, capturing both candid emotions and sweeping views of the shoreline. From the joyful smiles to the breathtaking coastal scenery, the images reflect the beauty of Florida as well as the warmth of Larkin and Kate’s love story.

For couples traveling from near or far, Larkin and Kate’s wedding is a perfect example of how Florida’s beaches—paired with expert planning by Vows By The Sea and the creative vision of Anna Parker Photography—create unforgettable destination weddings.
