Fort De Soto Beach Wedding - Jamie & Sally

August 14, 2025 · Fort De Soto Beach · St. Petersburg, Florida

Jamie & Sally’s dream wedding at Fort De Soto Beach was a perfect blend of romance, natural beauty, and Florida sunshine. Nestled along the Gulf Coast near St. Petersburg, Fort De Soto offers powdery white sands, crystal-blue waters, and breathtaking sunsets—the perfect backdrop for saying “I do.”

The couple’s ceremony took place just steps from the shoreline, with gentle waves creating a serene soundtrack. Their décor was understated yet elegant—soft neutral tones, fresh florals, and a delicate arch framing the ocean horizon. The relaxed coastal vibe allowed their personalities and love story to take center stage.

Why Choose Fort De Soto for Your Florida Beach Wedding

Fort De Soto isn’t just a stunning location—it’s also versatile, offering secluded spots for intimate elopements or spacious beachfronts for larger celebrations. Couples love this Gulf Coast gem for its:

  • Unmatched Views: Sunset over the Gulf is truly magical.
  • Natural Beauty: Soft dunes, palm trees, and pristine sands.
  • Convenient Location: Close to St. Petersburg and Tampa Bay.
