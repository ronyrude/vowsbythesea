Sand Key Beach Wedding - Moli & Jose

August 15, 2025 · Sand Key Beach · Clearwater, Florida

Moli and Jose’s wedding at Sand Key Beach was a breathtaking celebration of love, elegance, and Florida’s coastal charm. Organized by Vows By The Sea, every detail of their special day was designed to highlight the natural beauty of Sand Key’s white sands and stunning Gulf of Mexico backdrop.

The intimate beach ceremony unfolded under clear skies and soft ocean breezes, creating a romantic atmosphere for the couple to exchange vows. As the sun dipped into the horizon, Sand Key Beach revealed its signature golden and pink sunset, adding a magical touch to their celebration.

Tom Matthew Photography captured the day with artistry and precision, from emotional candid moments to sweeping views of the shoreline. Each image reflects the joy of Moli and Jose’s union, the stunning natural surroundings, and the thoughtful planning that brought their vision to life.

For couples dreaming of a Florida destination wedding, Moli and Jose’s Sand Key celebration is a perfect example of how the right team—expert planning by Vows By The Sea and timeless imagery by Tom Matthew Photography—creates memories that will be cherished forever.
