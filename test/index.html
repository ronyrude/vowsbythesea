Florida Beach Wedding FAQs

Get quick answers to the most common questions couples ask when planning a Gulf Coast celebration.

How far in advance should we reserve our Florida beach wedding?

Secure your preferred sunset time 6–12 months out for peak season (February–June and October). We can also help with short-notice elopements when dates are open.

Do your packages include photography and officiants?

Yes. Every package features a licensed officiant and professional photography. Premium collections add extended coverage, video, and album-ready galleries.

Can we customize decor or add a reception celebration?

Absolutely. Choose from bamboo arches, tiki torches, live musicians, and beach-friendly receptions. Our planners coordinate vendors and manage set-up and strike.

Do you handle permits and weather back-up plans?

We obtain permits for every beach location and prepare indoor or covered alternatives when needed. Rain plans are reviewed with you the week of the wedding.
