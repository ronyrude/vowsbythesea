Quick Intro

We coordinate around lifeguard zones and foot traffic to secure a ceremony spot with space and light.

Parking & Timing Tips

  • Large on-site lot - fills quickly on peak weekends; arrive early.
  • Weekdays are calmer - target 60-90 minutes before sunset.
  • Allow extra time during spring break and holidays.

Typical Guest Counts

  • Ideal for 2-50 guests. Seating can be added to most packages - we will recommend best layouts for this access.

Best Times of Year

  • Fall through spring: cooler temps and pastel skies.
  • Summer: plan around afternoon showers and heat index.

Photography Pointers

  • Use the boardwalk and shoreline for variety in portrait angles.
  • Schedule a 10-15 minute couple session immediately after vows.

Nearby Amenities

  • Restrooms, showers, and concessions on site.
  • Restaurants nearby for a quick post-ceremony celebration.
