On-Site Ceremonies: Hotel, Resort, Airbnb & Private Property

We’ll bring the arch, décor, audio, and photo to your location. Use this checklist so everything is smooth with the venue, HOA/condo, and local rules.

1) Permission & point-of-contact

  • Get written approval from the hotel/resort/host/HOA for a beach or lawn ceremony setup.
  • Share the on-site contact so we can coordinate load-in and quiet hours.

2) Location & access

  • Exact ceremony spot (beach, lawn, rooftop, private sand access).
  • Nearest parking/loading and route to the setup area (elevators/boardwalks).
  • Power availability (we also carry battery audio).

3) Rules to respect

  • Noise limits, glass restrictions, décor rules (stakes/flames).
  • Guest number limits and chair allowances in common areas.
  • Public beach behind a hotel may still require a city/county permit—we’ll advise.

4) Weather & back-up

  • Have a covered option (ballroom, patio) ready just in case.
  • We monitor wind/tide/surf and adjust layout for comfort and safety.

What we provide on-site

  • Arch & décor per your selected package (plus aisle accents if included).
  • Officiant with custom vows and license guidance.
  • Portable audio for music and vows.
  • Photography coverage per package.
  • Full setup and clean-up within venue rules and timelines.
