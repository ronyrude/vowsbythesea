Parking & Timing Tips

  • Large county lots — arrive with buffer on weekends.
  • Target 60–90 minutes before sunset for photos.
  • Have a light-jacket plan in breezy months.

Typical Guest Counts

  • Good for 2–50 guests. Seating can be added to many packages; ask for layouts suited to Sand Key.
