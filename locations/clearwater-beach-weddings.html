Quick Intro

Popular zones include the Sand Key side and lifeguard-tower stretches (crowd and tide dependent). We will propose the calmest spot for your date.

Parking & Timing Tips

  • Large main lots — fill quickly on weekends; arrive early.
  • Weekdays are calmer — schedule 60–90 minutes before sunset.
  • Add buffer for spring break and holiday traffic.

Typical Guest Counts

  • Ideal for 2–40 guests. Seating can be added to most packages — ask for our Clearwater layouts.

Best Times of Year

  • Oct–Apr: Cooler temps, colorful sunsets, lighter humidity.
  • May–Sep: Warmth and dramatic skies; plan around afternoon showers.

Photography Pointers

  • Golden-hour portraits on the waterline; tower backdrops add scale.
  • Plan a 10–15 minute couple session immediately post-vows.

Nearby Amenities

  • Restrooms and showers near main accesses.
  • Boardwalk and restaurants for a quick toast after the ceremony.
